Tobacco Nights

"Tobacco Nights" eau de parfum by Kappu is a captivating unisex fragrance which blends the richness of tobacco with sweet apricot, warm cinnamon, creamy tonka bean, and luxurious cognac. It's similar to enjoying a pleasant evening by the fireside, but with added sophistication and appeal. Suitable for people who want a fragrance that is both comfortable and appealing, ensuring a lasting impression wherever you go.

Iris Breeze

“Iris Breeze” parfum by Kappu is a subtle yet intriguing unisex fragrance that blends the timeless elegance of iris with the soft muskiness of ambrette. Ripe pear gives the scent a refreshing sweetness, and ambroxan and musk form a warm, enveloping foundation. This fragrance conveys a sense of natural beauty and grace, making it ideal for those who value effortless sophistication.

Price: $95

Citrus Dreams

“Citrus Dream” parfum by Kappu is a scent that combines the smell of spring's first bloom with the bright flavor of sun-kissed citrus groves. It begins with the delicate delicacy of apple blossom and the energizing glitter of lemon, enticing the senses with a fresh and radiant atmosphere. As the perfume progresses, Iso E Super adds depth and mystery, while ambroxan provides a warm and sensual appeal. The velvety embrace of musk rounds out the mix, leaving a lingering trail of refinement and mystery. Each note harmonizes in "Citrus Dream," creating an exquisite olfactory journey that evokes dreams of limitless possibilities and eternal elegance.

Price: $95
